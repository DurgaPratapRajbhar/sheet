Traction Motors - Maintenance Guidelines

(As per Railway Board L/no. 2008/Elect(TRS)/113/5 Pt. Dated 08/12/2016)

Concerned shop: 9A/Traction Motor Sec.

SL Traction Motors Present status/Remarks
1 Flash over of Traction Motors can be minimized by following methods:
A) Condition of arching studs/horns should be closely monitored for their gaps & proper shape. Strictly it should be changed in case of flashing sign or change in shape.
B) Use of anti tracking varnish, cleaning of BHRR, proper spring tension of carbon brushes, proper use of carbon brushes etc should be maintained and keep proper record.
2 During overhauling ovality checking during commutator turning, proper mica under cutting & proper chamfering of commutator should be done.
3 Condition of inter connecting leads, terminal board & insulators should be checked thoroughly during Overhauling.
4 All insulating materials, insulators and other such items should be procured only from approved sources.
